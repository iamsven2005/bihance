## General Questions

### 1. What is Bihance?

**Bihance** is a tech-driven startup focused on enhancing productivity and providing innovative solutions for businesses and individuals. Our mission is to empower users with tools that simplify complex tasks, improve efficiency, and drive growth.

### 2. How did Bihance get started?

Bihance was founded by two students who met as executive committee members of the entrepreneurship club at their university. Sharing a common vision of increasing productivity for student startups, they launched Bihance to provide solutions that help businesses and individuals enhance their operations.

### 3. What does "Bihance" stand for?

The name **Bihance** is derived from the concept of "enhance." The "B" represents our business, while the "h" stands for "hance," symbolizing our commitment to helping our users grow and enhance their capabilities.
